About me

UX Specialist based out in Bucharest

Certified UX Designer (by the The International Usability and User Experience Qualification Board - UXQB) based in Bucharest , fast creative thinker with solid visual design skills and background, striving for efficiency, willing to go the extra mile to exceed customer expectations.
